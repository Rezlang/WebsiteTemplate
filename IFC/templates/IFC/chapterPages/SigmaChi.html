  Sigma Chi was originally founded at Miami University of Ohio in 1855. In December of 1950 the Delta Psi chapter of Sigma Chi was established here at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. Sigma Chi takes pride in being a fraternity to help develop “leaders of men” and holds true to its beliefs of friendship, justice and learning. The brothers of the Delta Psi chapter stick true to their values and enjoy giving back to the community through events such as leaf rake and the Haunted House.
