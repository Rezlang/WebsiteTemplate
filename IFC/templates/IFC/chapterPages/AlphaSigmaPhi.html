Alpha Sigma Phi

  Alpha Sigma Phi is a fraternity with brothers that strive to follow their motto “To Better The Man”. Always actively improving themselves, brothers will refuse to be stagnant. Following the 5 values: Silence, Charity, Purity, Honor, Patriotism; you can always count on Alpha Sigma Phi to be a place that anyone can enjoy and be greeted with a friendly face. A variety of activities, Monster sponsorship, personalities, and a house at 16 Belle Avenue brings brothers together to grow and have a 2nd family at RPI and for the rest of their lives.
